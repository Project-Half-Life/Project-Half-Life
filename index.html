Welcome to

Project Half-Life

Project Half-Life is a virtual hackathon competition designed to give the world hope and inspire the next generation of innovators to think of and create solutions to the worl'd's greatest challenges. Climate change, disease, politics, infrustructure, and much, much more. The biggest change that Half-Life brigs to virtual hackathons is the fact that we will bring the hackathon (and the experience that comes with it) to you. That's right. Food, swag, prizes, people, events, games, workshops, and more. This virtual hackathon brings the hacking spirit, the all-nighter energy, and nervous excitement to you.
